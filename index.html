Who We Are

Tru Heart, Inc. is a community based non-profit organization that strives to revitalize under served communities through volunteer efforts, community engagement and activism.

Our Mission

Is to serve and provide disadvantaged communities with informative programs, community service events, collaborative art projects, mentorship and family engagement opportunities.

Our Vision

Restoring value within broken communities and instilling hope of a better future for children and their families within the Capital Region.

Founded by

Khalid Miller, and brought to life by directors Paul Collins-Hackett, Kelly Jean-Charles, Jose Romero, Kimyah Jean-Charles, Candace Dobbs, and Jamel Mosely.
