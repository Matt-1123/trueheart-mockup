New Bistro

The owner of New World Bistro, Annette Nanes, held aJim’s Way restaurant night, in-which 10% of all proceeds made from 5-10pm went directly to Tru Heart, Inc! With the help of New World Bistro and those who came out to support during those hours, we raised $875!

Mission Accomplished Transition Services
