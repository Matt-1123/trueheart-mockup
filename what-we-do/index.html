Who We Are: Tru Heart, Inc. is a community-based non-profit organization that strives to create opportunities for youth from marginalized communities, in hopes to build them up through volunteer efforts, community engagement, activism. Our hope is that these youth will become successful by their own definition and will create opportunity for youth in their community

Our Mission: is to build future leaders through mentorship, volunteering and education.

Our Vision: Future leaders creating opportunities for themselves 

Founded by: Khalid Miller, and brought to life by executive board directors Paul Collins-Hackett, Kelly Jean-Charles, Kimyah Jean-Charles, Candace Dobbs-Miller, and Jamel Mosely.
